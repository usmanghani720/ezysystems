When you access DeckUltimate, we may automatically collect:

We do not store full payment card details. Payments are processed securely through third-party payment processors. We may store basic billing metadata for verification or account management.

4. Location Information (Optional)

If you enable location-based features and grant permission, we may collect and store your device’s location. You may enable or disable location access at any time.

We use cookies to improve your experience and service performance. These include:

Optional cookies can be disabled, but some features may not function fully. DeckUltimate does not respond to “Do Not Track” signals at this time.
